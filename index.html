A Poem for You

A Love Letter in Lines and Light

In the strokes of a pen, I’ve tried to show, A heart that’s yours, more than you know. Each line, each swirl, is a piece of me, Bound to the beauty in you I see. Your name etched in a timeless art, A symbol of the rhythm that guides my heart. The stars in your eyes outshine the night, And your smile, oh, it’s my guiding light. A simple "N," yet it holds my world, Where love and dreams are gently swirled. This gift, a canvas, a token, a sign, Of a love so pure, it’s simply divine. You are the sunset’s gentle hue, The softest shade of every view. And as I walk this maze called life, Your warmth cuts through every strife. This Valentine, I give you my heart, In every drawing, every written part. For words and art are all I can give, To show the joy in the life I live.

Thank You

Words can't fully express how much I appreciate you and how grateful I am to have you in my life. You are kind, smart, beautiful, and an all-around an amazing person. I love you, and no one has my heart like you do, no one is on my mind like you are. I can’t find the right words to capture just how much you mean to me.
